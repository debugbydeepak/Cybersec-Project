How It Works

Unlike traditional scanners that rely on static regex patterns, SECUREWAY operates as a **Cognitive Security Agent**. It doesn't just look for bugs; it understands the intention of your code. By combining Playwright's headless execution with Engine-driven reasoning, it interacts with your application exactly like a human attacker—poking at business logic, attempting BOLA/IDOR escalations, and tracing SSRF callbacks in real-time.
